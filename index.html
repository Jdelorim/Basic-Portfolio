About Me


Joshua deLorimier is a new media artist, software developer and interactive motion graphics designer. Due to his passion for the real-time integration of sound and music, Joshua has crafted a career around the design of audio reactive visuals and artwork for live stage concert situations. Joshua has designed and performed visuals with acclaimed musicians spanning multiple genres. He creates custom software designs with mathematical algorithms to produce immersive interactive experiences with the accompanying music. He is trained formally in motion graphics and film editing from Wilmington University in Wilmington, DE and Video Symphony in Burbank, CA.


Joshua works fluently with 3D packages and 2D design tools. He builds custom modified software designed specifically to handle his unique and meticulous workflow. Joshua believes that the tools used in the production of creative work are as influential to the process as the artist themselves. In the last year, he has expanded his portfolio with numerous works including an installation entitled “Algorithmic Structures” which was displayed at a local venue in Newark, Delaware. Joshua has recently begun working on mobile platforms and is currently concentrating on developing virtual reality software.
